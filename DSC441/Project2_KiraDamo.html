Data Explanation

The dataset comprises breath alcohol tests conducted in Ames, Iowa, a university town. Each row corresponds to a person tested.

The main variables include:

  • year: The year the test was conducted.
  • month: The month the test was conducted.
  • day: The day the test was conducted.
  • hour: The hour the test was conducted.
  • location: The police department where the test was conducted.
  • sex: The sex of the person tested.
  • Res1: The first breath alcohol content measurement (%).
  • Res2: The second breath alcohol content measurement (%).

20. Create a new column “time_of_day” that categorizes tests into “Morning”, “Afternoon”, “Evening”, and “Night”. Then count tests in each category.

  • Hint: Use case_when to define time categories based on hour.

Periods:

  • Morning: 6 AM to 11:59 AM
  • Afternoon: 12 PM to 5:59 PM
  • Evening: 6 PM to 11:59 PM
  • Night: 12 AM to 5:59 AM
df |>
    mutate(time_of_day =
        case_when(
            hour >= 6 & hour < 12 ~ "Morning",
            hour >= 12 & hour < 18 ~ "Afternoon",
            hour >= 18 & hour < 24 ~ "Evening",
            hour < 6 | hour == 24 ~ "Night"
        )
    )
